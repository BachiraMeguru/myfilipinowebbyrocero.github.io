1. DEMOGRAPH OF THE PHILIPPINES




The total population of Philippines is 106,512,074 people. People in Philippines speak the English,Filipino language. The linguistic diversity of Philippines is very diverse according to a fractionalization scale which for Philippines is 0.836. The median age is approximately 23.5 years. Life expectancy in Philippines is 69. The female fertility rate in Philippines is 2.9. Around 6% of the population of Philippines are obese. The ethnic diversity is almost uniform according to a fractionalization scale which for Philippines is 0.2385.
